Project 1
Data Analysis of Top 100 TikTokers in 2025

Data Analysis of Top 100 TikTokers in 2025 This project explores the performance and trends of the top 100 TikTok influencers in 2025 by analyzing critical data points like followers, likes, upload frequency, and user engagement. By examining how these influencers attract and maintain large audiences, the project uncovers key factors contributing to viral success. The insights gained help in understanding content strategies, user interaction patterns, and the overall influence of TikTok personalities in shaping social media trends.

Project 3
Sweet Treat Sales 2024 Dash Board Project

In this project, I Leveraged SQL to extract and organize global sales data, used Excel for data cleaning and preliminary analysis, and developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to visualize key metrics such as total sales amount, boxes shipped, product performance, and regional trends. Implemented DAX measures such as Total Sales = SUM(Amount), Total Boxes Shipped = SUM(Boxes Shipped), and Sales by Product/Country/Salesperson to drive dynamic insights. The project enables clear visualization of sales distribution across countries, products, and sales personnel..
